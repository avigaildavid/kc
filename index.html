I deliver front-end javascript and angular consulting services without all the drama.

Here is what I promise all of my customers and students.

  • You will receive friendly service.
  • You will pay a fair rate.
  • You will be happy.

That is the way I do business.

You tell me what you need. I'll do it for you. That's it.

When my cable TV stops working, I don't want a long-winded explanation about cable TV technology. In the same manner, I don't believe in beating people up with a lot of techno mumbo jumbo. I'm the nerd; I'll take care of that stuff.

I do believe in building websites that are standards-compliant and cross-browser compatible. Why? Because not everyone will view your site on the exact same kind of computer that you use, nor will they always have a high-speed internet connection, flash or sound. Flashy stuff can sometimes look cool and serve a purpose, but a clean and fast loading web site is usually to your advantage. This approach will also help a great deal with SEO (i.e. how people find you to search the web).
